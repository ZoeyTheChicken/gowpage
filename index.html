The Gears of War server began as a creative Minecraft server on February 18, 2023, before transforming into one of the most dynamic survival nations servers. Over its 17-month lifespan, the server witnessed the rise and fall of empires, devastating wars, complex alliances, and player-driven geopolitical drama that would ultimately culminate in its dramatic conclusion in July 2024.

Ownership of the server changed hands five times throughout its history, passing from its original creator Tig through several players before ultimately landing with Minishyft6243, who held it for the server's final and longest stretch.

Server Ownership

The Gears of War server changed ownership five times over its lifespan. The server was passed between players at various points, reflecting the shifting power dynamics and activity levels on the server.

Nations

A comprehensive listing of all nations that existed on the Gears of War server, ranked by their historical significance and impact.

#1

Caveville

Founded: February 21, 2023
Capital: Caveville (formerly East Caveville)
Leaders: Zoey, Ice, Squad
Status: Destroyed (July 2024)

The most dominant and longest-lasting nation on the server. Originally started as a small settlement after the server switched to survival mode, it quickly expanded and split into East Caveville (the original) and Caveville (the expansion). Won the Astro-Cavevillian War and likely Cold War I. After the Reichpakt collapse on March 4, 2023, absorbed all former Reichpakt territories, becoming a superpower. By April 1, 2023, one of only two major nations remaining with approximately 3 members. Engaged in a 13-month silent conflict with Flareia before being destroyed in the final war.

Wars Participated
  • Astro-Cavevillian War (Victor)
  • Cold War I (Likely Victor)
  • Flareia VS Caveville War I
  • Flareian-Caveville War II (Destroyed)
#2

Flareia

Founded: Early 2023
Leader: Minishyft6243
Status: Destroyed (July 2024)

The second major superpower on the server and Caveville's primary rival. By April 1, 2023, Flareia had approximately 5 members, giving it a numerical advantage over Caveville. Flareia was allied with Caveville during Cold War I against the Reichpakt. After the war, the alliance broke down, leading to the first Flareia-Caveville War started by Minishyft in March 2023. Despite being bombed in both major wars, Flareia recovered from the first conflict and remained a dominant force until the server's end.

Wars Participated
  • Cold War I (Allied with Caveville)
  • Flareia VS Caveville War I
  • Flareian-Caveville War II (Destroyed)
#3

Deutschland

Founded: February 25, 2023 (Reformed from Asgrothia/Petram)
Former Names: Asgrothia, Petram
Leader: CraftedYT
Status: Destroyed (March 4, 2023)

Originally founded as Petram by CraftedYT on February 23, 2023, branching off from East Caveville. After the Astro-Cavevillian War defeat, Asgrothia separated from Petram, and both nations reformed into Deutschland. Deutschland became a founding member of the Reichpakt alliance on March 1, 2023. The nation was wiped from the server when the Reichpakt collapsed on March 4, 2023, with its territories absorbed by Caveville.

Wars Participated
  • Astro-Cavevillian War (As Asgrothia - Defeated)
  • Cold War I (As part of Reichpakt - Defeated)
#4

E

Founded: Early 2023
Leader: Pumice
Status: Active until server end

A small but strategically important nation owned by Pumice. E was closely allied with Caveville throughout most of the server's history. During Cold War I, E was part of the Caveville alliance while Pumice simultaneously commanded the opposing Reichpakt forces, creating a unique political situation. E participated in the Astro-Cavevillian War alongside Caveville.

Wars Participated
  • Astro-Cavevillian War (Allied with Caveville)
  • Cold War I (Allied with Caveville)
#5

Asgrothia

Founded: February 23-24, 2023
Leader: CraftedYT
Status: Reformed into Deutschland (February 25, 2023)

Originally part of Petram, Asgrothia was the aggressor in the first major server conflict. The Astro-Cavevillian War lasted only one day (February 24-25, 2023) and ended in defeat. After the war, Asgrothia separated from Petram and both nations reformed into Deutschland. The nation's defeat marked the beginning of CraftedYT's controversial reputation on the server.

Wars Participated
  • Astro-Cavevillian War (Defeated)
#6

Petram

Founded: February 23, 2023
Leader: CraftedYT
Status: Reformed into Deutschland, then destroyed (March 4, 2023)

The original nation founded by CraftedYT by branching off from East Caveville. Immediately declared war on Caveville upon founding. After the Astro-Cavevillian War, reformed into Deutschland alongside Asgrothia. Became part of the Reichpakt alliance before being wiped from the server during the alliance's collapse.

Wars Participated
  • Astro-Cavevillian War (As part of Asgrothia)
  • Cold War I (As part of Reichpakt via Deutschland)
#7

Texas

Founded: 2023
Capital: Austin
Leader: Dino
Status: Small nation, active until server end

A small but notable nation founded by Dino. Texas maintained its independence throughout the server's history with Austin serving as its capital. While not involved in major conflicts, Texas represented the smaller nations that existed alongside the superpowers of Caveville and Flareia.

#8

Cherokee

Founded: Before March 1, 2023
Status: Destroyed (March 4, 2023)

A member nation of the Reichpakt alliance. Cherokee joined the alliance alongside Deutschland, E, and Petram on March 1, 2023. The nation participated in Cold War I on the Reichpakt side. When the Reichpakt collapsed on March 4, 2023, Cherokee was wiped from the server along with all other alliance members.

Wars Participated
  • Cold War I (As part of Reichpakt - Defeated)
#9

Canada

Founded: February 27, 2023
Capital: Quebec
Leader: Herodelombre
Status: Small nation

A small nation founded by Herodelombre on February 27, 2023. Canada established Quebec as its capital and remained neutral in most server conflicts. Represented one of the minor powers that existed during the early server period.

#10

Munich

Founded: February 25, 2023 (Post Astro-Cavevillian War)
Status: Unknown

Munich was established as one of the consequences of the Astro-Cavevillian War. The nation emerged alongside Deutschland after the conflict ended. Limited information is available about Munich's activities or ultimate fate.

Timeline

A chronological record of major events in the Gears of War server's history.

February 18, 2023

Server Launch: Gears of War server started as a Minecraft creative server owned by Tig. Minishyft6243 and ZoeyDev left spawn without Tig noticing and used /fill command to spawn 20,000 TNT blocks in a mountain several hundred blocks from spawn.

February 21, 2023

Survival Mode Switch: Server converted to survival mode. Caveville (later known as East Caveville) was founded. The settlement quickly expanded, with the newer area becoming known simply as "Caveville" while the original became "East Caveville."

February 23, 2023

Petram Founded: CraftedYT branched off from East Caveville and created the nation of Petram on the opposite side. War was immediately declared on Caveville.

February 24-25, 2023

Astro-Cavevillian War: First major server conflict. Asgrothia (CraftedYT) fought against Caveville/E (Zoey, Ice, Squad). War lasted only one day. Caveville emerged victorious. Asgrothia separated from Petram after defeat.

February 24-26, 2023 (estimated)

4th Reich-Caveville Conflict: A concurrent conflict began alongside the Astro-Cavevillian War. Deutschland fought against Caveville in a poorly-documented conflict. Estimated to have ended in a draw around February 26, 2023. Very little information survives about this war.

~February 26, 2023

Ownership Transfer ‚Äì Tig ‚Üí Ice: Tig passed ownership of the server to Ice around this time. Ice would go on to serve as owner during some of the server's earliest turbulent conflicts.

February 25, 2023

Post-War Reformation: Following the Astro-Cavevillian War, Petram and Asgrothia reformed into Deutschland. Munich was also established as a consequence of the war.

February 27, 2023

Canada Founded: Herodelombre, a small player, formed the nation of Canada with Quebec as its capital.

March 1, 2023

Reichpakt Alliance Formed & Cold War I Begins: Deutschland joined with Pumice to form the Reichpakt alliance. Members included: Cherokee, Deutschland, E, and Petram. Cold War I immediately began between the Reichpakt (plus Horizon Confed) and Caveville & Flareia. Zoey commanded Caveville forces while Pumice commanded the Reichpakt. CraftedYT was widely blamed for starting the conflict.

March 3, 2023

Cold War I Ended: Two-day conflict concluded. Caveville & E (with Flareia support) believed to have won, though official outcome unclear.

March 4, 2023

Reichpakt Collapse: The Reichpakt alliance fell apart. All member nations (Cherokee, Deutschland, E, and Petram) were wiped from the GOW server. Caveville absorbed all former Reichpakt territories, solidifying its position as the dominant superpower.

~March 2023

Ownership Transfer ‚Äì Ice ‚Üí Herodelombre: Ice passed ownership of the server to Herodelombre sometime in March. Herodelombre, already the founder of Canada, would hold the server through the lead-up to the first major Flareia-Caveville conflict.

March 18, 2023

Flareia VS Caveville War I Begins: Minishyft6243 started a war between Flareia and Caveville "for no reason at all, just for fun." This marked the beginning of the great rivalry between the server's two remaining superpowers.

April 1, 2023

Two-Nation System: Flareia and Caveville established as the only two major powers. Caveville had approximately 3 members while Flareia had approximately 5 members, giving Flareia the numerical advantage in the arising conflict.

Early April 2023

CraftedYT Quits: One month after the Reichpakt collapse, CraftedYT permanently left the server.

~April 2023

Ownership Transfer ‚Äì Herodelombre ‚Üí Zoey: Herodelombre passed ownership to Zoey around this time, shortly after CraftedYT's departure. Zoey, already the leader of Caveville and one of the server's most prominent figures, took on the role of server owner.

April 18, 2023

Flareia VS Caveville War I Ended: After two months of conflict, the war concluded. Both nations were heavily bombed but both recovered from the damage. The war ended inconclusively with no clear victor.

April 2023 - June 2024

Silent Conflict Period: For 13 months, Flareia and Caveville maintained a tense cold peace. Both superpowers continued to build strength while avoiding direct military confrontation.

~June 2023

Ownership Transfer ‚Äì Zoey ‚Üí (Unknown): Zoey's ownership ended around June 2023. There is a gap of roughly two months before the next confirmed owner. It is unclear who, if anyone, held ownership of the server during this period.

~August 2023

Ownership Transfer ‚Äì (Unknown) ‚Üí Minishyft6243: Minishyft6243 became the server's 5th and final owner around August 2023. He would hold ownership for nearly 11 months ‚Äî the longest tenure of any single owner in the server's history.

June 15, 2024

Flareian-Caveville War II Begins: After 13 months of silent conflict, Minishyft6243 started another war between Flareia and Caveville. This final conflict would prove to be the most brutal in server history.

June-July 2024

War Crimes Era: The final war featured numerous atrocities including horse hostage situations, extensive griefing, widespread theft, and systematic destruction of both nations' infrastructure.

July 12, 2024

Server End: Flareian-Caveville War II concluded with both nations completely destroyed. With both superpowers in ruins and players having lost interest, the Gears of War server officially ended after 17 months of history.
